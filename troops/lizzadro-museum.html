Lizzadro Museum of Lapidary Art

Description:

Tour the Lizzadro Museum. The general tour includes an introductory presentation about earth science, focusing on the difference between rocks and minerals, how rocks form, fossils, and the rock cycle. Following the presentation, the Troops will have the opportunity to explore the Museum using a scavenger hunt as a tool to see the exhibits. This can take as short or long a time as you’d like. Typically, it takes kids 40-60 minutes to see the entire museum.

Directions:

The Lizzadro Museum of Lapidary Art is located at the corner of Jorie Blvd and Kensington Road, Oak Brook, Illinois, 17 miles west of downtown Chicago.

Parking:

Please park on the east side of the parking lot. Parking is Free. Enter the Museum through the main entrance at the south end of the building. Please check in at the front desk upon arrival. Let them know you are with the St. George Group. Tours begin in the Museum Lecture Hall and students will be directed by Museum Staff.
